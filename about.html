About Us

About LiberiaCalc

Last updated: June 2026

LiberiaCalc is a free financial tool website built for the Liberian people. Our goal is simple: to give every Liberian access to fast, easy-to-use calculators that help with everyday money decisions — whether that's converting Liberian Dollars to US Dollars, checking mobile money transfer fees, or understanding a loan repayment plan.

Too often, financial tools online are built for Western markets and ignore the realities of doing business in Liberia. LiberiaCalc exists to change that.

💡 LiberiaCalc is proudly connected to LiberiaMedia.com — Liberia's independent news platform. Reliable. Independent. Trusted.

What We Offer

  • LRD to USD Converter — Convert Liberian Dollars to US Dollars and back, with a custom rate input so you can use the latest street or bank rate.
  • Mobile Money Fee Calculator — Estimate transfer fees for Lonestar Mobile Money and Orange Money before you send.
  • Loan Interest Calculator — Calculate your monthly repayments and total interest on any loan amount.

Accuracy Disclaimer

All calculations on this site are estimates for informational purposes only. Exchange rates and mobile money fees change regularly. Always confirm with your bank, the Central Bank of Liberia (CBL), or your mobile money provider before making financial decisions.

Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information LiberiaCalc collects, how we use it, and your rights as a user of this website.

1. Information We Collect

LiberiaCalc does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment details. All calculations on this site happen entirely within your browser — no data you enter into any calculator is sent to our servers.

2. Automatically Collected Data

Like most websites, LiberiaCalc may automatically collect non-personal information when you visit, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile or desktop)
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring website (how you found us)
  • General geographic location (country/region level only)

This information is collected through cookies and analytics tools to help us understand how people use the site and improve it over time.

3. Google AdSense & Advertising

LiberiaCalc uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to show ads that are relevant to you based on your browsing activity. These cookies are set by Google, not by LiberiaCalc.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data, visit: How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

4. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. LiberiaCalc uses cookies for:

  • Remembering your preferences (such as a custom exchange rate you entered)
  • Analytics — understanding how visitors use the site
  • Advertising — serving relevant ads through Google AdSense

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect how some parts of this site function.

5. Third-Party Links

LiberiaCalc may contain links to external websites such as LiberiaMedia.com or the Central Bank of Liberia. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

LiberiaCalc does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this site, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

8. Your Rights

Since LiberiaCalc does not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions about data collected by third-party services like Google, please refer to their respective privacy policies.
